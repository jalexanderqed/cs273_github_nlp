Just a note about typo on https://github.com/facebook/rocksdb/wiki/Performance-Benchmarks.

 --compression_ratio=50

The above appears to be a bad option setting.  Should be 

 --compression_ratio=0.5

The use of 50 produces values that will not compress.  The timing of tests changes dramatically with proper option setting.  See db_bench.cc for verification.  Default is setting to 0.5.  Settings parsed via:
